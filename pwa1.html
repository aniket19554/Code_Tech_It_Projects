Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LuxuryShop PWA

How does the offline shopping experience work?

Our Progressive Web App uses advanced caching techniques to store product information, images, and your shopping cart locally on your device. When you lose connectivity, you can continue browsing previously viewed products, add items to your cart, and save favorites. Once you're back online, all changes are automatically synchronized with your account.

Is LuxuryShop PWA secure for making purchases?

Absolutely. All transactions are processed through our secure payment gateway with end-to-end encryption. The PWA is served over HTTPS, ensuring all communications between your device and our servers are protected. We also implement the latest security standards to safeguard your personal and payment information.

How do I install LuxuryShop on my device?

On mobile devices, simply visit our website using Chrome, Safari, or Edge. You'll see an "Add to Home Screen" prompt (or you can find it in your browser menu). On desktop, look for the install icon in the address bar (Chrome/Edge) or in the Safari menu. Once installed, it will launch like a native app with its own icon and window.

Can I receive notifications without installing the app?

Yes! Even without installing, you can opt-in to receive web push notifications about exclusive offers and updates. However, for the best experience including offline access and background notifications, we recommend installing the PWA to your device.
